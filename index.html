Who We Are

A Foundation Built on
Compassion & Action

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla. We are a Section 8 registered non-profit licensed by the Central Government, headquartered in Chhattisgarh and operating across India.

Our work spans education, food security, women's empowerment, environmental conservation, animal welfare, and youth skill development — driven by a belief that collective action creates real, lasting change.

Section 8 Registered 80G & 12A Certified CSR-1 Registered NITI Aayog ISO 9001:2015

"A charity should focus on children, animals, women, and the environment. Every contribution is an act of dignity and respect."

— Mr. Govind Shukla, Founder & CEO

Our Initiatives

Six Projects.
One Mission.

Each project is designed to address a critical gap in society — from hunger and illiteracy to environmental damage and unemployment.

🍱
Project SEVA
Food & Clothing Relief

Distributing meals, clothing, and essential supplies to the homeless and underprivileged across India. No one should sleep hungry.

✅ 50,000+ meals & clothing items distributed
📚
Project BACHPANSHALA
Education for Every Child

Bridging educational gaps by providing underprivileged children with digital literacy training, life skills support, and school education assistance.

✅ Empowering hundreds of children with digital skills
🦋
Project UDAAN
Women Empowerment

Equipping women with vocational skills, financial independence, and professional development opportunities to help them soar beyond limitations.

✅ Women-led livelihood creation across multiple states
🌿
Project PRAKRITI
Environmental Conservation

Driving sustainability through tree plantation drives, eco-awareness campaigns, and community-led environmental conservation programs.

✅ Thousands of saplings planted pan-India
🐾
Project JEEV
Animal Welfare

Rescuing, feeding, and protecting stray and vulnerable animals. Because compassion for all living beings is the mark of a just society.

✅ Regular animal feeding & rescue drives across cities
💼
Project VIKAS
Skill Development

Building youth employability through professional internship programs, digital skill training, and career development across India.

✅ 30,000+ interns trained and mentored
